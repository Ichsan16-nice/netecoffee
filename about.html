HOW DID THIS BUSINESS START?

In a big city surrounded by buildings, someone who loves coffee, dreams of creating a place to enjoy quality coffee. With small capital and great enthusiasm, he learned directly from coffee farmers, and he was inspired to grow "nete".

That person transformed an old building into a warm and friendly coffee shop. Nete's opening day brought hope, and in no time, the place became a favorite. Customers come not only for the coffee, but also for the intimate atmosphere.

Working closely with farmers, The Guy ensures they get fair prices. Nete ​​is a symbol of dreams that come true with love and hard work, a place where coffee brings together simple stories and memories.
